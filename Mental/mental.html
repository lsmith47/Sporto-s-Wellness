Wellness

Practicing good wellness, along with physical health, allows you to thrive in life rather than just survive. Using wellness skills, we are able to control how we handle stress, relate to others, and make healthy choices. Through practicing good mental health coping skills, you are able to be more creative, try and learn new things, and make healthy choices.
