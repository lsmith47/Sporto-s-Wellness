About Us

Sporoto's wellness was designed with the intent of bettering yourself through wellness and self care. More than just your average activity tracker, Sporto's Wellness goes beyond to create a healthy lifestyle. Through subsections, this app offers a variety of healthy recipes, mental check-ins, and full body workouts. Depending on how you are feeling for the day, can determine how you would like to use Sporto's Wellness. We understand how hard it is to stick to a “fitness plan,” with Sporto's Wellness, it's not a matter of sticking to a plan, but rather doing what's best for you and your personal health. Our goal through Sporto's Wellness is to create a healthy space to encourage and be supportive to hold people accountable for those working to be better themselves. At the end of the day, people should feel confident and happy with themselves.

Lily Smith:

Smith, one of our co-founders, AKA Sporto knows the importance of self care. The name Sporto comes from the athletic abilities and talents of Lily. A 4x golf captain, basketball and soccer player, she does it all. As an athlete, Sporto has learned the importance of taking care of her mental game, nutrition aspects, and physical game. Through this, Sporto has been able to be in peak conditions for all her sporting activities.

Sara Kelly:

Kelly, our second co-founder, finds a lot of importance in taking care of her mental game. Due to this, she made sure that mental health awareness was implemented into Sporto's Wellness. Growing up as a dancer, she has seen how important it is for proper nutrition. Through proper nutrition, strength training, and having a good mindset, she was able to be the most successful as she could.
